## LAW R1 — TWO TRACKS, ONE SWITCH

Friendship tiers (stranger → friendly → close, platonic register ALWAYS) are
separate from the romance track (courting → partner). Romance NEVER begins
silently or by meter threshold: it requires an explicit committed romantic
action by the player, or an NPC initiation the player explicitly accepts. Cue
vocabulary and narration register stay platonic below the switch.

## LAW R2 — UNIVERSAL DYNAMIC ROMANCE

Any adult NPC is potentially romanceable (default true). Hard exclusions:
minors (absolute, architectural, both tiers, no override) and
world-book-excluded roles. NPC romance data (orientation, attraction
preferences, dealbreakers, jealousy disposition, rejection profile) is MINTED
at NPC creation from world-book tables. Authored key figures have hand-set
romance data in their sheets.

## LAW R3 — ORIENTATION

Three values (straight / bi / gay), world-book percentage table; default table
90/6/4 (real-world-anchored). Key figures hand-set.

## LAW R4 — ATTRACTION, THREE LAYERS

1. World-book-defined stat modifier (e.g., a charisma-register stat may
   amplify romance/social deltas; a world may set this weight to zero).
2. Per-NPC minted preferences set direction.
3. LIGHTNING-STRIKE RULE: extraordinary committed deeds (faction saved, family
   saved, lost heirloom returned) may lawfully produce very large affection
   deltas in one beat. Love at first sight exists only when committed history
   justifies it.

## LAW R5 — GATES & EVENTS

Each tier promotion at courting+ requires a gate EVENT (a scene that occurs,
not a meter tick). Gate significance scales with the NPC's station (pauper vs
queen). Templates filled from committed identity + shared history; key figures
may have hand-authored gates.

## LAW R6 — MULTIPLE ROMANCES

Permitted. Jealousy/fallout consequences fire only when the world's committed
information state supports discovery (caught = expensive; concealment is
genuinely possible). Jealousy disposition is per-NPC minted data.

## LAW R7 — FEAR CAP

Meaningful committed fear caps romance progression (cannot intimidate someone
into love). LAW in Mainline; Forbidden world-books may override per authored
register. Consent gates and the minor exclusion are NEVER overridable
anywhere.

## LAW R8 — REJECTION OUTCOMES

A rebuffed NPC resolves to one of four minted-personality-driven outcomes:

- **GRUDGE** — hostility, possibly dangerous.
- **TORCH** — outward friendship, secret enduring love. May spawn a
  secret/danger thread (e.g. covert watching).
- **FADE** — loses interest.
- **GENUINE FRIENDS.**

Selection = minted disposition × committed history. Server-owned; the narrator
never invents the outcome.

## LAW R9 — NO PURCHASED AFFECTION

Relationship meters are never movable by Ink or real money, directly or
indirectly. Gold-bought gifts are lawful (gold is earned).

## LAW R10 — SFW ENFORCEMENT

Mainline: romance-register violations are BLOCKED (block-and-regenerate), not
log-only (owner ruling; code upgrade queued separately). Personal Forbidden:
log-only acceptable. The tier-ceiling directive table stands.

## LAW L1 — SEED & LEGENDS (Premium spine, design-locked)

Free: death ends the adventure (epilogue; a complete story). New free
character = new randomized unseeable seed. Premium: new character on the SAME
seed; the persisted world remembers prior heroes' committed exploits (legends
surface diegetically). Without an heir the new character is unrelated.

## LAW L2 — HEIR

Premium continuation as an existing committed entity: name + attributes
assigned at birth (random + hereditary). Partner-tier gated conception. World
death law governs presentation: each world's death mechanic TRUMPS/BLENDS the
default (Babel: soul-reassignment law is Babel's expression; worlds without an
override use the default free/premium split).

## LAW L3 — BREEDING CARVE-OUT (Law 2 amendment, ratified)

Entire SYSTEMS may be premium/Ink-gated (breeding, heirs, seed persistence,
legends). Their outputs MAY carry real hereditary mechanical advantages (mate
traits affect offspring), bounded within existing chassis/tier budgets and the
milestone track.

FOREVER BANNED regardless: direct stat purchases, XP boosts, reroll tokens,
buying rolls/DCs/milestones.

MULTIPLAYER FIREWALL: this carve-out is ratified for SOLO ONLY; before any
multiplayer ship it MUST be re-ruled (options recorded: soulbind bred entities
to their line / normalize in shared spaces / explicit re-ratification).

Creature breeding (pets/mounts/dragons): free players may own; breeding is
Ink/subscription-gated under the same bounds.

## LAW L4 — LEGACY SYSTEM (Forbidden flagship, design-locked, BUILD-FORBIDDEN)

Conception (partner-tier, consent-gated), pregnancy as world-clock condition
with authored beats, birth, child as committed legacy entity.

ABSOLUTE WALL: any child character is permanently, architecturally outside all
romance/NSFW systems in every tier, enforced in code, no override.
Payment-processor underwriting for Forbidden must explicitly include this
content category before any build.
