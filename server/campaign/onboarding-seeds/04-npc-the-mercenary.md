This is a role, not a fixed person. Whoever fills it sits with their back to stone and their face to the room, one hand never far from a hilt. Old scars and old debts come with the role; the specific name, face, and history are generated fresh for each run.

The mercenary has worked for smugglers, pilgrims, and once for captains tied to [[The Ashen Watch]]. None of those stories ended clean. They claim to want no part of [[The Missing Shipment]], but they flinch when certain route markers are named, and they owe the keeper of [[The Shattered Flagon]] more than they admit.

Behavioral hooks: this role can become a blade at the player's side or the blade sent after them. Much depends on whether [[{{characterName}}]] speaks to the person they are now or the weapon they used to be.
