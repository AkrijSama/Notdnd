[[The Blight]] is what the town calls the sickness in the woods, but no one agrees on whether it is a plague, a curse, or a warning. Trees bend inward where it spreads, bark blackens like old wounds, and birds stop singing long before you see the first rot.

Travelers report hearing familiar voices deeper in the thicket, always just out of sight. Hunters return with blank memories or not at all. Those who do return speak of light moving against the wind.

The official line from [[The Ashen Watch]] says it is superstition. The empty farms beyond [[Ashenmoor]] say otherwise. If [[The Missing Shipment]] touched Blight-marked ground, this town's fear is only beginning.
