[[The Ashen Watch]] began as a wall between the town and the wild roads. Now they are a wall between the town and the truth. Their captains take coin for "protection audits," and their patrols somehow miss every crime that matters.

After [[The Missing Shipment]] vanished, the Watch closed the gate logs, silenced stable hands, and announced the road "unsafe for civilians." In practice, that means no witnesses and no questions.

Some guards are merely frightened. Some are bought. A few are true believers in whatever order is coming behind [[The Blight]]. Either way, anyone looking for answers in [[Ashenmoor]] will eventually collide with them.
