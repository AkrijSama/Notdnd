The rain never truly stops in [[Ashenmoor]], but it sounds loudest on the cracked slate roof of The Shattered Flagon. The tavern smells of wet wool, lamp oil, and iron-rich blood that no amount of soap has ever quite erased.

[[Mira]] keeps the room warm and the questions careful. [[Drek]] drinks in the shadows near the hearth, pretending not to watch the door. [[The Whisperer]] sits at the back table where the candlelight refuses to settle.

Tonight the same story moves from cup to cup: wagons vanished, guards paid to look away, and the road gone hungry. Every version points toward [[The Missing Shipment]], and every version ends by lowering its voice when someone says [[The Blight]] aloud.

When [[Kael]] steps inside, the room measures them in one long heartbeat.

- a cracked slate roof hammered by rain
- soot‑blackened beams
- wet wool, lamp‑oil, old blood scent in the air
- shadows pool in corners where hearth’s fire sputters
- scarred wooden tables
- low temperature
- low overhead light from candles
