[[Ashenmoor]] was built where old trade roads meet marsh fog and bad decisions. Timber walls lean under years of smoke and rain, and every alley keeps a memory of boots running in the dark.

Most travelers first learn the town through [[The Shattered Flagon]], where news arrives before mercy. The bell tower rings for curfew now, not prayer. The people say it changed when [[The Ashen Watch]] started collecting "extra duties" at spearpoint.

The missing caravan has put everyone on edge. Mothers pull children indoors at dusk. Merchants nail doors shut from inside. No one trusts the official story about [[The Missing Shipment]], and no one likes how the tree line whispers with [[The Blight]] after midnight.
