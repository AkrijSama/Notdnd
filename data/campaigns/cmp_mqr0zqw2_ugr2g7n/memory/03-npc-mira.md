[[Mira]] runs [[The Shattered Flagon]] with a patient smile and a knife hidden where only she can reach it. She remembers names, debts, and lies with equal precision. Strangers call her kind. Locals call her careful.

She has seen [[Ashenmoor]] survive bad winters, plague wagons, and men in uniforms who said they were here to help. She does not trust the calm silence from [[The Ashen Watch]] about [[The Missing Shipment]].

Mira's voice can soothe a frightened room, but it hardens when anyone mentions the forest roads after dark. She knows more about [[The Blight]] than she admits, and she studies [[Kael]] like someone deciding whether to hand over a secret or a warning.
