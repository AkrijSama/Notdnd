How It Works

Upload your world, launch into live play, and keep the story alive between sessions with persistent companion chat.

Upload Your World

Drop in your homebrew notes, lore docs, or start from a template. The AI parses everything and builds a playable campaign in minutes.

Play With an AI GM

The AI runs your sessions live. It narrates, roleplays NPCs, manages combat, and tracks everything. It calls for checks, rolls initiative, and describes consequences. Up to 4 players in real-time.

It Never Forgets

Every NPC conversation, every quest detail, every player backstory builds a living knowledge graph. Between sessions, chat with the world. Ask NPCs what they think of you. Write backstory. Plan your next move.

Built for Persistent Campaigns

Multiple AI Models

Choose between frontier models for the smartest GM or free models to keep costs zero. OpenRouter gives you access to Grok, GPT, Gemini, Llama, and more.

Uncensored Storytelling

Dark fantasy, horror, moral complexity, romance. Your story, your rules. No content filters gatekeeping your narrative.

Real-Time Multiplayer

Up to 4 players connected via WebSocket. See each other's actions live. Combat turns enforced automatically.

Companion Mode

Between sessions, talk to the AI one-on-one. Chat with NPCs you've met. Write backstory collaboratively. Plan strategy. The world is always alive.

Homebrew First

Upload markdown, text, or JSON. The parser extracts entities, indexes them, and makes them playable. Your world, not ours.

Zero Prep GMing

5-minute quickstart: upload files, generate a full campaign package with scenes, encounters, maps, tokens, and journals. Launch directly to the VTT.
